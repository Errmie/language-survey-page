Kosovar Diaspora & European Integration Survey

This survey is part of a research collaboration between the Southern University of Denmark and Volt Kosovo, a branch of Volt Europa. Our study aims to understand how members of the Kosovar diaspora in Europe balance their cultural heritage while integrating into their host societies.

Your insights on language use, cultural participation, identity, and perspectives on the EU’s policies towards Kosovo are invaluable. By participating, you contribute to research on cultural integration and heritage preservation that can benefit future generations and inform policies.

For any questions regarding the project, please contact Ermal Asllani at ermal.asllani@volteuropa.org.
